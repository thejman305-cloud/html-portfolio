About Me

I am a Senior Product Designer with over six years of experience designing complex digital products. My work primarily focuses on solving real-world problems in financial and procurement systems, including purchase orders, invoices, approvals, and accounting workflows. I enjoy working on problems that require both structured thinking and creativity, especially when the systems involved are complex and interconnected.

As a designer, I am particularly interested in turning ambiguity into clarity. I like breaking down messy product problems, identifying the core user needs, and designing solutions that are intuitive, scalable, and trustworthy. I work closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders to ensure that design decisions align with both user needs and business goals.

Outside of my day-to-day design work, I am passionate about continuous learning and creative exploration. I am interested in building a personal brand around product design, sharing ideas about design thinking and product strategy, and exploring creative interests such as music and content creation. I enjoy reflecting on how design, technology, and creativity intersect to shape meaningful experiences.
