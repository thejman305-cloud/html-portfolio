The best movies according to Jyothiswar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Princess Mononoke

All ghibli studio movies are incredibly well made. This movie focusses on how humans affect the wildlife and nature. I love how artistically and creatively the story is built around the concept of man versus nature

Pursuit of Happyness

Yes, the title of this movie has a typo in the word "Happiness" and it is intentional. To understand this one has to watch the movie. This movie is about how hard work and early struggles can pay off in the long term. Loved this movie.

I am Legend

This movie is about a zombie apocalypse. It was surreal to see how the creators had imagined a post apocalyptic earth.
